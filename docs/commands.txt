EZVAC ALPHA 1.0 - COMMAND REFERENCE
===================================

All commands use /hvac. /climate is an alias.


BASIC COMMANDS (ezvac.use)
------------------------------

/hvac help
  Shows the commands available to the sender.

/hvac temperature
/hvac temp
  Reports local temperature, outdoor temperature, the influencing vent group,
  airflow-path distance, and core/leakage status.

/hvac list
  Lists HVAC systems in the player's current world with mode, room/target
  temperature, and running/registered equipment counts.

/hvac info
  Looks up the targeted thermostat, equipment unit, vent, thermometer, RPM
  monitor, or settings panel within six blocks.


CREATE DEVICES (ezvac.admin)
--------------------------------

/hvac create thermostat <id> <group> [targetF]
  Look at a sign. Creates the only thermostat/controller for that group in the
  sign's world. IDs and group labels are 1-32 letters, numbers, '_' or '-'.

/hvac create heatpump <group>
/hvac create ac <group>
/hvac create furnace <group>
  Look at a dispenser. Registers typed equipment in the specified group.

/hvac create thermometer [label]
  Look at a sign. Creates an independent point sensor at that block. It needs no
  group and may be placed outdoors; its reading matches /hvac temperature there.

/hvac create rpmmonitor <group> <heatpump|ac|furnace> [label]
  Look at a sign. Matching typed equipment must already exist in the group.

/hvac create settingspanel <group> [label]
  Look at a sign. Creates the group's single operating-profile panel in NORMAL.
  The group must already contain a thermostat or equipment.

SETTINGS PANEL USE (ezvac.use)
--------------------------------

- Right-click advances NORMAL -> TURBO -> ECO -> NORMAL.
- Sneak-right-click moves in the opposite direction.
- ECO defaults to a 2,400 RPM cap, NORMAL to 3,600, and TURBO to 4,200.
- Profiles affect every heat pump and AC in the group in sync.
- Furnaces remain fixed at full heating capacity in every profile.
- Removing or losing the panel returns the group to NORMAL automatically.


THERMOSTATS (ezvac.admin)
-----------------------------

/hvac thermostat <id> set <temperatureF>
  Sets and safely clamps the target to the configured target range.

/hvac thermostat <id> enable
/hvac thermostat <id> disable
  Enables or fails the thermostat safe to idle.

/hvac thermostat <id> group <group>
  Moves the thermostat to another group in the same world. The destination may
  not already have a thermostat.

/hvac thermostat <id> info
  Shows room/target, mode, group, and enable state.

Thermostat signs may also be edited. Put a finite number on line 3. Non-admin
players cannot modify registered thermostat signs; thermometer, RPM, and
settings-panel signs are always read-only through the sign editor.


EQUIPMENT AND GROUPS (ezvac.admin)
--------------------------------------

/hvac equipment enable
/hvac equipment disable
/hvac equipment group <group>
/hvac equipment info
  Look at registered equipment. Group changes first stop and clean the old
  output; the change is refused while cleanup is waiting on an unloaded chunk.

/hvac group <group> enable
/hvac group <group> disable
/hvac group <group> info
  Controls or inspects the named group in the player's current world.


VENT LINKING (ezvac.admin)
------------------------------

/hvac tool
  Gives the persistent HVAC Duct Link Tool.

1. Right-click a registered dispenser to select its group.
2. Right-click iron trapdoors in the same world to link them as vents.
3. Sneak-right-click a registered vent to unlink it.

The selected group must still contain equipment when a vent is linked.


REMOVAL AND DIAGNOSTICS (ezvac.admin)
-----------------------------------------

/hvac remove
  Removes the targeted registered device. Equipment retracts its owned output,
  or persists cleanup until the output chunk loads. Removing a settings panel
  returns its HVAC group to NORMAL.

/hvac stats
  Reports device totals and airflow cache/rebuild/cap statistics.

/hvac sync
  Queues one coalesced zero-time authoritative reconciliation. This changes no
  simulation time; it immediately applies command/block/world state changes.


INSTALL / UPGRADE
-----------------

1. Stop the server and back up the existing plugins/EZvac directory, if present.
2. Put EZvac-ALPHA-1.0.jar in the server plugins directory.
3. Start Paper 1.21.1 on Java 21.
4. Confirm that EZvac enables without errors.
5. Use /hvac stats and /hvac list, then test one full heating and cooling cycle.
